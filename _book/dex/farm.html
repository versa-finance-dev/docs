💸 Farm

The Farm is the place to stake your LP tokens (which you can get by providing Liquidity) and earn VERSA as a reward.

Supported pairs

  • ASTR-VERSA
  • ASTR-BUSD
  • ASTR-USDC

Farming

So how do you farm? It's very easy:

  1. Select a pair you want to farm

2. Get some LP tokens by providing liquidity for the pair you selected.

3. Earn VERSA!
